How do you handle conflict?

Fifteen questions to reveal which of the five conflict management styles you reach for most.

There are five core conflict management styles: Competing, Collaborating, Avoiding, Accommodating, and Compromising. No style is better than another — each has pros and cons, and each can be useful depending on the situation.

This assessment helps you identify your typical response to conflict, so that in future conflicts you'll be aware of your instinctive reaction, its pros and cons, and the other styles you could draw on instead.

For each statement, choose how often it described you in the past 6 months, from 1 (rarely) to 5 (always). Be honest — there are no right or wrong answers.

Adapted from the Thomas-Kilmann Model and Reginald (Reg) Adkins, Ph.D., Elemental Truths Blog.
